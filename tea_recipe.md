# How to Make Tea

Making a cup of tea is a simple process, typically involving these steps:

1.  **Boil Water:** Fill a kettle with fresh water and bring it to a boil.
2.  **Warm the Teapot/Mug (Optional but Recommended):** Pour a small amount of boiling water into your teapot or mug, swirl it around, and then discard it. This helps keep your tea hot.
3.  **Add Tea:** Place a tea bag or loose-leaf tea into your warmed mug or teapot. A general rule for loose leaf tea is one teaspoon per 250 ml (8.4 fl oz) of water.
4.  **Pour Water:** Pour the freshly boiled water over the tea.
5.  **Steep:** Allow the tea to steep for the recommended time. This varies by tea type (e.g., 3-5 minutes for black tea, 2-3 minutes for green tea).
6.  **Remove Tea:** Take out the tea bag or strain the loose leaves.
7.  **Customize (Optional):** Add milk, sugar, or honey to taste.
8.  **Enjoy:** Stir and enjoy your tea.
